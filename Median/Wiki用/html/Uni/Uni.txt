		<p class="genbig">UNIQUE ITEMS</a></span></p>
		<p class="content">There are 475 unique items in Median XL 1.86, 471 in earlier versions.</p>
		<hr />
		<p class="content"><b>Normal Uniques</b><br />
			Most unique items can exist in Tier 1 to Tier 6 versions. Higher tier
			versions of the same unique have similar stats, but are improved in all areas. See
			the <a href="items.html" タージt="tekst">BASE ITEMS</a> page for details on item tiers.</p>
		<p class="content"><b>Sacred Uniques</b><br />
			Additionally, the sacred tier of each item type has its own unique version.
			Sacred uniques can only drop in <u>レベル 121 areas</u> and nowhere else.</p>
		<p class="content"><b>Unique アミュレットs, リングs, Quivers and Jewels</b><br />
			These item types have no tiers. Most unique アミュレットs, リングs, quivers and
			jewels can be found in the regular game, though the very best only spawn in
			<u>レベル 121 areas</u>.</p>
		<hr />
		<p class="content"><b>* </b>The following areas are レベル 121 on Destruction (Hell) difficulty :</p>

			レベル 121, though the chance of finding a レベル 121 unique item this way is
			fairly small.</p>
		<hr />
		<p class="content"><b>個のソケット</b><br />
			All unique items come with the maximum allowed number of 個のソケット for the item
			type.</p>
		<p class="content"><b>Oskills</b><br />
			If a unique has a skill bonus with no character class requirement (eg.
			<span class="magic">+3-6 to Summon Shadows</span>), then the bonus is an 'oskill',
			giving this skill to any class for free.</p>
